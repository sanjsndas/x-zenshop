Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

+

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days ($5.99 or free for orders over $50)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($12.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($24.99, order by 12PM PST)

Processing time is typically 1-2 business days. During peak seasons, please allow additional processing time.

What is your return policy?

+

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@xzenshop.com
  2. We'll provide you with a return authorization number and instructions
  3. Package your item securely and include the original packing slip
  4. Ship the package back to us using a trackable method

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

How do I care for my xZenShop plushies?

+

To keep your plushies looking and feeling their best:

  • Machine Wash: Most plushies can be washed in cold water on gentle cycle. Use mild detergent.
  • Drying: Air dry is recommended. If using a dryer, use low heat and remove promptly.
  • Spot Cleaning: For small stains, use a damp cloth with mild soap.
  • Brushing: Use a soft-bristle brush to restore fur texture.
  • Storage: Keep in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Always check the care tag on your specific product for detailed instructions.

Are your products safe for children?

+

Yes, safety is our top priority. All xZenShop products are:

  • Made with child-safe, hypoallergenic materials
  • Free from harmful chemicals like BPA, phthalates, and lead
  • Tested to meet or exceed all U.S. and international safety standards
  • Designed with securely attached parts to prevent choking hazards

Each product has age recommendations based on safety considerations. We recommend adult supervision for children under 3 years old.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

+

Yes! We love working with retailers, schools, hospitals, and other organizations. Our wholesale program offers:

  • Discounts starting at 30% off retail for qualifying orders
  • Custom branding options for bulk orders
  • Dropshipping services available
  • Flexible minimum order quantities

To learn more about our wholesale program, please email wholesale@xzenshop.com with your business details and requirements.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account on our website (if you created one)
  2. Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  3. Contacting our customer service team with your order number

Please note that tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear after your order ships.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International orders are subject to:

  • Shipping costs calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight
  • Possible customs fees, duties, or taxes which are the responsibility of the recipient
  • Longer delivery times (typically 7-21 business days depending on destination)

Some restrictions may apply based on destination country regulations. Please contact us before ordering if you have specific questions about shipping to your country.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Shop Pay
  • xZenShop Gift Cards

All payments are processed securely through our encrypted checkout system. We never store your full payment information on our servers.

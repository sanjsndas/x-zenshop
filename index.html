Our Story

xZenShop was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves comfort. In a world that can often feel overwhelming, we believe in the power of softness to bring calm and joy.

Founded in 2018 by two childhood friends who bonded over their love of plush toys, xZenShop has grown from a small Etsy shop to a beloved brand serving thousands of customers worldwide.

Each of our plushies is carefully selected or designed with three principles in mind: Softness, Sweetness, and Serenity. We work with ethical manufacturers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our ZenCare Promise

At xZenShop, your satisfaction is our top priority. We stand behind every product we sell and want you to be completely happy with your purchase.

Quality Assurance: Each plush undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards for softness, durability, and safety.

Easy Returns: Not completely satisfied? We offer a 30-day return policy with no questions asked.

Safe Materials: All our products are made with child-safe, hypoallergenic materials that are free from harmful chemicals.

Washable Design: Most of our plushies are machine washable (check individual product care instructions).

Customer Support: Our friendly support team is available 7 days a week to answer any questions or concerns.

We believe in the power of comfort and strive to create products that bring joy and relaxation to your life. If for any reason we fall short of your expectations, please let us know so we can make it right.
